Comprehensive Open Source ERP Offering the Best School Management Software for Institutes.

Our Education ERP software is designed to manage the day to day activities or operations of Educational Institutions efficiently and effectively. It includes essential functionalities required to manage schools efficiently and automate everything remotely such as:-

  • Student information and Record keeping.
  • Staff and Faculty Management.
  • Academic Evaluation and Gradebook Generation.
  • Accounting, Finance and Multiple Online Payment methods
  • Library Management and Digital library
  • Automated Reminders via sms/email
  • Payroll Management
  • Parent Login and Discplinary tracking

Elimu Pal School Management System assits the management in taking data-driven and result oriented decisions with achieving long-term goal.

Analytics

The ERP discovers, interprets and communicates significant patterns in student and indiviual subject performances and this analysis helps teachers understand their students' learning abilities and challenges and also guage their performance as teachers

Exam Evaluation and Gradebook

Our education ERP software also assists teachers to record grades electronically, calculate averages, automatic student grading, indivial gradebook generation, class/stream performance sheet generation and they can access data on a student's performance in both online and physical programs and assessments.

Financial Management

Elimu Pal School Management System assists in financial accounting to prepare payrolls and in maintaining a school's financial records through income from student fee and any other internal resources and in managing expenditure

Student Data Management

The ERP offers easy collection, analysis and storage of data assisting in proper keeping of records and ease in data retrieval.
